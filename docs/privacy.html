Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 24, 2026

At WishAPaw, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile information
  • Pet Information: Pet names, photos, and wish list items
  • User Content: Wishes, notes, and other content you create

Information Collected Automatically

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app and its features
  • Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the WishAPaw service
  • Personalize your experience
  • Send you notifications about your pet's wishes (if enabled)
  • Improve our app and develop new features
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption and security practices:

  • Data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • We use secure cloud infrastructure (Supabase) with enterprise-grade security
  • Access to personal data is strictly limited and monitored
  • Regular security audits and updates

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted third-party services that help us operate the app (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With Your Consent: When you explicitly authorize us to share information

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: View your personal data at any time through the app
  • Correction: Update or correct your information in the app settings
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Export: Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt-Out: Disable notifications and analytics in app settings

6. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

WishAPaw is designed to be family-friendly and safe for users of all ages. We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • Parents can review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information
  • We do not require children to provide more information than necessary to use the app
  • If we discover we have collected information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will delete it immediately

7. Third-Party Services

WishAPaw uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: For secure data storage and authentication
  • Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics (you can opt out in settings)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending an in-app notification for material changes
