🐾 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Our Commitment

WishAPaw is designed to support children during medical treatment. We take your privacy and your child's safety very seriously.

Information We Collect

WishAPaw currently stores all data locally on your device:

  • Treatment journey progress
  • Mood check-in history
  • Paws earned and courage cards unlocked
  • App preferences and settings

No personal information is sent to external servers at this time.

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

WishAPaw is intended for use by children under the supervision of a parent or caregiver. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.

Parents and caregivers should review this policy and supervise their child's use of the app.

Important: All data remains on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally using iOS UserDefaults. Data remains on your device and is included in device backups (iCloud or iTunes).

Medical Disclaimer

WishAPaw is NOT a medical app and does not provide medical advice. It is a supportive companion tool. Always consult with your healthcare provider for medical decisions.

Data Deletion

You can delete all app data at any time by:

  • Using the "Reset All Progress" button in Settings, or
  • Deleting the app from your device

Third-Party Services

WishAPaw does not currently use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date will reflect when changes were made. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. You can:

  • View all stored data within the app
  • Delete all data through the Settings menu
  • Remove the app entirely from your device
